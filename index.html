Why choose us?

Founded in 2018, Noonray Energy Pvt. Ltd., is a technology-focused company offering specialized solutions in renewable energy, electric mobility, and the automotive sector. One of its core areas include technical advisory, through which, Noonray supports clients in making informed technology and investment decisions while optimizing system performance and cost efficiency.

The company also provides engineering services designed to enhance the reliability, safety, and efficiency of modern energy and vehicle systems. By combining simulation-based insights with practical validation, Noonray ensures that its solutions are both technically robust and economically viable.

Noonray is also deeply invested in upskilling through a range of technical training programs. Through these initiatives, Noonray helps build skilled professionals equipped to meet the evolving challenges of the energy and mobility industries.
